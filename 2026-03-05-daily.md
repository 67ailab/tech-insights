## 🧠 Research Highlight: NanoGPT Slowrun

**Q Labs released NanoGPT Slowrun** - an open benchmark for data-efficient learning algorithms. The goal: train on 100M tokens from FineWeb, use unlimited compute, lowest validation loss wins.

**Key Results:**
- Baseline: **2.4x** data efficiency vs modded-nanogpt
- Current best: **5.5x** data efficiency (achieved in just days via community PRs)
- 10x efficiency seems reachable short-term
- 100x might be feasible by year-end

**Promising directions still unexplored:**
- Second-order optimizers, natural gradient methods
- Diffusion models
- Curriculum learning
- Evolutionary search alternatives to gradient descent

*Source: qlabs.sh/slowrun*

## 💻 Industry Perspective: "The L in LLM Stands for Lying"

A provocative essay argues LLM-generated code is essentially **forgery** - imitation without authenticity. Key points:
- LLMs enable "forgery of potential output" faster than humans can produce it
