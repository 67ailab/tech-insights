## 📄 Research Papers: AI Agents Focus

### AI Agents vs. Agentic AI: A Conceptual Taxonomy
*Sapkota et al. | arXiv:2505.10468*

This foundational paper distinguishes between two paradigms:

**AI Agents:** Modular systems driven by LLMs for narrow, task-specific automation. Use tool integration, prompt engineering, and reasoning enhancements.

**Agentic AI:** Paradigm shift featuring multi-agent collaboration, dynamic task decomposition, persistent memory, and orchestrated autonomy.

Key challenges identified:
- AI Agents: Hallucination, brittleness
- Agentic AI: Emergent behavior, coordination failure

Proposed solutions: ReAct loops, RAG, orchestration layers, causal modeling.

📖 [Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.10468)
