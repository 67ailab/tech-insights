## 📄 Top Hugging Face Papers (AI/Agents)

### 1. RadAgent: A Tool-Using AI Agent for Stepwise Interpretation of Chest CT
- **Upvotes:** 5 | **Authors:** Mélanie Roschewitz, Kenneth Styppa, Yitian Tao, Jiwoong Sohn, et al. (ETH Zurich)
- **URL:** https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.15231
- **Project:** https://rad-agent.github.io
- **Abstract:** Vision-language models have advanced AI-driven medical imaging interpretation, but existing methods offer no interpretable reasoning trace. RadAgent is a tool-using AI agent that generates CT reports through a stepwise, interpretable process with fully inspectable traces of intermediate decisions and tool interactions. Results show 6.0 point improvement in macro-F1 clinical accuracy, 24.7 point improvement in robustness under adversarial conditions, and 37.0% faithfulness—a new capability absent in 3D VLM counterparts.

### 2. LongAct: Harnessing Intrinsic Activation Patterns for Long-Context Reinforcement Learning
- **Upvotes:** 5 | **Authors:** Bowen Ping, Zijun Chen, Tingfeng Hui, Qize Yu, et al.
- **URL:** https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.14922
- **Abstract:** Proposes LongAct, a strategy exploiting high-magnitude activations in query/key vectors for long-context reasoning. Shifts from uniform to saliency-guided sparse updates, achieving ~8% improvement on LongBench v2. Works across RL algorithms (GRPO, DAPO), suggesting focusing on salient features unlocks long-context potential in LLMs.

### 3. OneHOI: Unifying Human-Object Interaction Generation and Editing
- **Upvotes:** 6 | **Authors:** Hoe Jiun Tian et al. (Nanyang Technological University)
- **URL:** https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.14062
- **Project:** https://jiuntian.github.io/OneHOI/
- **GitHub:** https://github.com/jiuntian/OneHOI
- **Abstract:** Introduces OneHOI, a unified diffusion transformer framework consolidating HOI generation and editing into a single conditional denoising process. The Relational Diffusion Transformer (R-DiT) models verb-mediated relations through role- and instance-aware HOI tokens, layout-based spatial Action Grounding, and Structured HOI Attention. Achieves SOTA across both HOI generation and editing tasks.

### 4. LeapAlign: Post-Training Flow Matching Models at Any Generation Step
- **Upvotes:** 5 | **Authors:** Zhanhao Liang, Tao Yang, Jie Wu, et al. (ByteDance Seed)
- **URL:** https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.15311
- **Project:** https://rockeycoss.github.io/leapalign/
- **Abstract:** Addresses alignment of flow matching models with human preferences. LeapAlign reduces computational cost and enables direct gradient propagation from reward to early generation steps by shortening trajectories into two-step leaps. Outperforms GRPO-based and direct-gradient methods on Flux model fine-tuning across image quality and image-text alignment metrics.
