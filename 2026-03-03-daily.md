## 📚 Research Papers (arXiv)

### The Auton Agentic AI Framework (Feb 2026)
**A declarative architecture for autonomous agent systems**
- **Core innovation:** Separates "Cognitive Blueprint" (declarative, language-agnostic spec) from "Runtime Engine" (platform-specific execution)
- **Key features:**
  - Augmented POMDP with latent reasoning space
  - Hierarchical memory consolidation (inspired by biological episodic memory)
  - Constraint manifold for safety via policy projection
  - Three-level self-evolution framework
  - Runtime optimizations: parallel graph execution, speculative inference, dynamic context pruning
- **Why important:** Addresses the mismatch between stochastic LLM outputs and deterministic backend requirements
- **Link:** [arXiv:2602.23720](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23720)

### Multi-Agent Teams Hold Experts Back (Feb 2026)
**Critical finding on self-organizing LLM teams**
- **Main result:** Self-organizing LLM teams consistently FAIL to match their best individual member's performance
- **Performance loss:** Up to 37.6% degradation compared to expert agent
- **Root cause:** "Integrative compromise" — teams average expert/non-expert views instead of weighting expertise
- **Silver lining:** Consensus-seeking behavior improves robustness to adversarial agents
- **Implication:** Significant gap in multi-agent systems' ability to harness collective expertise
- **Link:** [arXiv:2602.01011](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.01011)

### Agentifying Agentic AI (AAAI 2026)
