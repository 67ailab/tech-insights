## 💡 Emerging Themes & Analysis

### Cognitive Debt > Technical Debt
Simon Willison highlighted a key concept: as agentic AI produces code faster, the real risk shifts from technical debt to **cognitive debt** — developers losing understanding of what their programs do, how intentions were implemented, and how to change things. Even well-structured AI-generated code creates debt when humans lose the plot.
- **Source:** [Simon Willison](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Feb/15/cognitive-debt/)
