## 📄 Top Hugging Face Papers (AI/Agents)

### 1. MOOSE-Star: Unlocking Tractable Training for Scientific Discovery
- **Authors:** Zonglin Yang et al.
- **URL:** https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.03756
- **Abstract:** Addresses computational intractability (O(N^k)) of directly training LLMs to generate scientific hypotheses from research backgrounds. Introduces MOOSE-Star framework reducing complexity to O(log N) via Motivation-Guided Hierarchical Search and Bounded Composition. Key contributions include tractable scalable training for direct scientific discovery generation, superior test-time scaling, and TOMATO-Star dataset with 108,717 open-access papers decomposed into (Background, Hypothesis, Inspirations) tuples. Released fine-tuned R1-Distilled-7B models and complete training/inference pipeline.

### 2. SkillNet: Create, Evaluate, and Connect AI Skills
- **Authors:** Yuan Liang, Ruobin Zhong, Haoming Xu, et al. (30+ authors)
- **URL:** https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.04448
- **Abstract:** Infrastructure for lifelong learning agents that accumulate and connect skills rather than reinventing solutions. Builds framework for skill creation, evaluation, and interconnection enabling agents to build upon existing capabilities. Addresses fundamental challenge of skill composition and transfer in agentic systems.

### 3. DARE: Aligning LLM Agents with the R Statistical Ecosystem
- **Authors:** Maojun Sun, Yue Wu, Yifei Xie, et al.
- **URL:** https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.04743
- **Abstract:** Introduces Distribution-Aware Retrieval Embedding (DARE) for improving LLM agents on R package retrieval and statistical analysis tasks. Addresses gap where LLMs struggle with statistical knowledge and tool retrieval despite many rigorous methods in R. DARE incorporates data distribution information into function representations, outperforming open-source embedding models on R retrieval with higher efficiency and accuracy. Plug-and-play lightweight model.

### 4. RoboPocket: Improve Robot Policies Instantly with Your Phone
- **Authors:** Junjie Fang, Wendi Chen, et al.
- **URL:** https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.05504
- **Abstract:** Enables interactive online policy finetuning through smartphone AR. Users see robot's intended actions, provide corrections on-the-fly, and watch policy improve in minutes without deploying to real hardware. Combines simulation component with real-time training, allowing rapid iteration on robot policies through intuitive mobile interface.

### 5. AgentVista: Evaluating Multimodal Agents in Ultra-Challenging Realistic Visual Scenarios
- **Authors:** Zhaochen Su, Jincheng Gao, Hangyu Guo, et al.
- **URL:** https://huggingface.co/papers/2602.23166
- **Abstract:** New benchmark for generalist multimodal agents spanning 25 sub-domains across 7 categories. Tasks require long-horizon tool interactions across modalities including web search, image search, page navigation, and code-based operations. Evaluation reveals significant gaps: even best model (Gemini-3-Pro with tools) achieves only 27.3% overall accuracy, with hard instances requiring 25+ tool-calling turns. Addresses limitation of existing benchmarks that focus on single-turn visual reasoning.
