## Key Takeaway

The discourse is splitting into two camps: builders shipping real agent infrastructure (HF Skills, smolagents, local agents) vs. critics questioning whether "agentic AI" is meaningful at all. The practical middle ground — agents as interoperability drivers and tool-builders — seems to be where the real value is landing.
