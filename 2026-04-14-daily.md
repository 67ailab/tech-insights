- **Authors:** Shibo-UCSD et al.

- **Abstract:** Proposes MEDS (Memory-Enhanced Dynamic Reward Shaping) for RL training of LLMs. Addresses recurring error patterns by tracking mistakes across training iterations. ### 2. QuanBench+: A Unified Multi-Framework Benchmark for LLM-Based Quantum Code Generation
- **Authors:** Ali Slim, Haydar Hamieh, Jawad Kotaich et al. (American University of Beirut, KAUST)
- **URL:** https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.08570
- **Abstract:** Introduces QuanBench+, a unified benchmark spanning Qiskit, PennyLane, and Cirq with 42 aligned tasks for evaluating LLM quantum code generation. Best one-shot scores: 59.5% (Qiskit), 54.8% (Cirq), 42.9% (PennyLane). With feedback-based repair: 83.3%, 76.2%, 66.7%. Demonstrates progress but highlights framework-dependency challenges for agentic code generation.

### 3. Attention Sink in Transformers: A Survey on Utilization, Interpretation, and Mitigation
- **Authors:** Zunhai Su et al. (Tsinghua, Meituan, HKU, etc.)
- **URL:** https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.10098
- **Abstract:** First comprehensive survey on Attention Sink (AS) phenomenon in Transformers, reviewing 180+ studies. Covers three dimensions: (1) Fundamental Utilization - exploiting AS for KV cache compression and efficient inference; (2) Mechanistic Interpretation - Outlier Circuits, Softmax limitations; (3) Strategic Mitigation - Gated Attention, Modified Softmax. Critical for understanding LLM internals affecting agent reliability.

### 4. OmniShow: Unifying Multimodal Conditions for Human-Object Interaction Video Generation

- **URL:** https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.11804
- **Abstract:** End-to-end framework for Human-Object Interaction Video Generation (HOIVG) unifying text, reference images, audio, and pose conditions. Introduces Unified Channel-wise Conditioning and Gated Local-Context Attention. Achieves SOTA across multimodal settings. Relevant for embodied AI agents and multimodal interaction.

### 5. Strips as Tokens: Artist Mesh Generation with Native UV Segmentation
