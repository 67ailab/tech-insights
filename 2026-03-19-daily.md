## 📚 Top Hugging Face Papers (AI/Agent Related)

### 1. Complementary Reinforcement Learning (23 upvotes)
- **Authors:** Dilxat Muhtar, Jiashun Liu, Wei Gao, et al. (Alibaba)
- **URL:** https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.17621
- **Abstract:** Reinforcement Learning for LLM-based agents remains limited by low sample efficiency. Complementary RL achieves seamless co-evolution of an experience extractor and policy actor within the RL optimization loop. The actor is optimized via sparse outcome-based rewards, while the experience extractor evolves based on whether its distilled experiences contribute to the actor's success. Achieves 10% performance improvement in single-task scenarios with robust scalability in multi-task settings.

### 2. Temporal Gains, Spatial Costs: Revisiting Video Fine-Tuning in Multimodal Large Language Models (14 upvotes)
- **Authors:** Linghao Zhang, Jungang Li, Yonghua Hei, et al.
- **URL:** https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.17541
- **Abstract:** Systematic study of how Video-SFT reshapes visual capabilities in MLLMs. Video-SFT reliably improves video performance but often yields limited gains or degradation on static image benchmarks. The trade-off is tied to temporal budget: increasing sampled frames improves video performance but doesn't reliably improve static image performance. Proposes instruction-aware Hybrid-Frame strategy to partially mitigate the image-video trade-off.

### 3. LoST: Level of Semantics Tokenization for 3D Shapes (10 upvotes)
- **Authors:** Niladri Shekhar Dutt, Zifan Shi, Paul Guerrero, et al. (Adobe)
- **URL:** https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.17995
- **Abstract:** Tokenization is critical for autoregressive 3D generation. LoST orders tokens by semantic salience, such that early prefixes decode into complete, plausible shapes with principal semantics, while subsequent tokens refine details. Uses Relational Inter-Distance Alignment (RIDA) loss to align 3D shape latent space with semantic DINO features. Achieves SOTA reconstruction using only 0.1%-10% of tokens needed by prior AR models.

### 4. CodeScout: An Effective Recipe for Reinforcement Learning of Code Search Agents

### 5. VideoAtlas: Navigating Long-Form Video in Logarithmic Compute
- **Authors:** Mohamed Eltahir, Ali Habibullah, Yazan Alshoibi, et al. (KAUST)
