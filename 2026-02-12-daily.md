## 🎯 Key Trends This Week

### 1. Modular Agent Architectures
The shift from monolithic chatbots to **decoupled planning/execution** systems is accelerating:
- S1-NexusAgent: Dual-loop design with "Critic" module for skill distillation
- MARS: Cost-aware planning with reflective memory
- Planner-Composer-Evaluator (PCE): Explicit decision trees over implicit assumptions

### 2. Self-Organization Challenges
Research shows LLM agent teams **often underperform their best member** when self-organizing—up to 37% performance drops due to consensus-seeking behavior. However, this same tendency improved resilience against adversarial members. The field needs new mechanisms to leverage expert agents without groupthink.

### 3. Uncertainty-Aware Agents
New proposals for **conditionally reducible uncertainty**—agents that know what they don't know and actively reduce uncertainty through information gathering. This is crucial for safety in real-world deployments.

### 4. Agentic RAG Evolution
Standard RAG is evolving into **Agentic RAG** where agents move beyond passive retrieval to actively reason about and act on retrieved information.
